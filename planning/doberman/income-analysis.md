## Risk Considerations

### Income Variability
- **Risk Level**: High
- **Mitigation**: Maintain 6-12 months living expenses in liquid savings
- **Consideration**: Trading income can vary significantly month-to-month

### Market Downturn
- **Risk Level**: Medium-High
- **Mitigation**: Diversify trading strategies, maintain emergency fund
- **Consideration**: Extended periods of low or negative returns possible

### Operating Cost Increases
- **Risk Level**: Low-Medium
- **Mitigation**: Lock in data feed contracts, use cost-effective infrastructure
- **Consideration**: Technology costs generally decrease over time

## Tax Implications

### Washington State Tax Advantages
- **No State Income Tax**: 0% on trading income
- **Capital Gains Tax**: Does not apply to short-term trading income
- **Business & Occupation Tax**: Applies to gross receipts at low rates
- **Reference**: Washington State Department of Revenue

### Estimated Tax Burden
- **Federal Income Tax**: Progressive rates on net income
- **Self-Employment Tax**: 15.3% on net self-employment income
- **Estimated Quarterly Payments**: Required for self-employed
- **Reference**: IRS self-employment tax guidelines
