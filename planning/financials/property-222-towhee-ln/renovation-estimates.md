# Renovation Estimates

## Overview

This document provides detailed estimates for renovating the existing shop building at 222 Towhee Ln, Brinnon, WA into a dual-purpose gym and workspace. The renovation includes structural modifications, insulation, electrical upgrades, HVAC installation, and aesthetic improvements.

## Property Context

- **Existing Structure**: Large shop building with additional small garage with office space above
- **Target Use**: Gym + workspace combination
- **Size Estimate**: 600-900 sq ft (typical for shop building)
- **Location**: Rural property with privacy and views
- **Year Built**: 1988 (existing home structure)

## Renovation Scope

### 1. Structural and Envelope Upgrades

#### Windows and Exterior Framing
- **Cost**: $15,000-20,000
- **Scope**:
  - Large windows for natural light (8-12 windows)
  - Structural headers and framing modifications
  - Energy-efficient windows with proper insulation
  - Permits and engineering fees
- **Reference**: HomeAdvisor average window installation costs
- **Details**:
  - Windows: $400-600 each (installed)
  - Structural modifications: $3,000-5,000
  - Engineering permits: $1,000-2,000
  - Weatherproofing: $2,000-3,000

#### Insulation (Walls + Ceiling)
- **Cost**: $5,000-8,000
- **Scope**:
  - Spray foam insulation for walls and ceiling
  - R-30+ insulation value for energy efficiency
  - Vapor barriers and moisture control
  - Labor and materials
- **Reference**: Spray foam insulation costs per square foot
- **Details**:
  - Spray foam material: $1.50-2.50/sq ft
  - Labor: $1.00-1.50/sq ft
  - Vapor barriers: $500-800
  - Total area: 1,200-1,800 sq ft

#### Exterior Siding Refresh
- **Cost**: $8,000-12,000
- **Scope**:
  - Match existing house aesthetic
  - Weather-resistant materials
  - Complete siding replacement
  - Trim and finishing work
- **Reference**: Siding replacement costs for rural properties
- **Details**:
  - Siding material: $5-8/sq ft
  - Labor: $3-5/sq ft
  - Trim and finishing: $1,500-2,500
  - Total area: 1,500-2,000 sq ft

#### Roofing Refresh
- **Cost**: $5,000-10,000
- **Scope**:
  - If existing roof needs replacement
  - Weatherproofing and sealing
  - New roofing materials
  - Labor and materials
- **Reference**: Roofing costs for structures of similar size
- **Details**:
  - Roofing material: $3-5/sq ft
  - Labor: $2-4/sq ft
  - Total area: 1,000-1,500 sq ft

## Cost-Saving Opportunities

### 1. DIY Opportunities
- Painting and finishing work
- Basic landscaping around the structure
- Simple furniture assembly
- **Potential Savings**: $5,000-10,000

### 2. Phased Implementation
- Phase 1: Essential structural and electrical work
- Phase 2: HVAC and insulation
- Phase 3: Interior finishes and features
- **Benefits**: Spread costs over time, adjust based on budget

### 3. Material Selection
- Use cost-effective but durable materials
- Consider refurbished or gently used equipment
- Shop for deals on bulk materials
- **Potential Savings**: $10,000-15,000

## Financing Options

### 1. Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC)
- **Advantages**: Flexible funding, interest-only payments
- **Considerations**: Variable interest rates, requires home equity

### 2. Personal Loan
- **Advantages**: Fixed rates, no collateral required
- **Considerations**: Higher interest rates, shorter terms

### 3. Renovation Loan
- **Advantages**: Can be included in mortgage financing
- **Considerations**: Requires additional underwriting

- **Risk Level**: Low-Medium

### 3. Weather Delays
- **Risk Level**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: Plan for weather contingencies, indoor work priorities
