- **Source**: Personal savings/investments
- **Reference**: Standard mortgage lending practices require 20% down for conventional loans

### 2. Closing Costs
- **Amount**: $12,750
- **Percentage**: 3% of purchase price
- **Breakdown**:

- **Amount**: $1,000-2,000

- **Reference**: National average inspection costs for rural properties
